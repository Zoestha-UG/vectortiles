Leipzig offers its residents and visitors a large number of new and established creative retailers, small factories and cafes, organic girls and co.

            

They are spread all over the city - in the small hidden studios, side streets and even larger highways of every neighborhood.

            

Zoestha would like to support and connect these retailers. Creating access for the clientele. As a central link between dealers, craftsmen and artists, they create added value for them and their customers.

            

We provide you with a map and a list of many interesting shops on this website. You can search for categories as well as freely within the city area.

            

 Anyone who has something unique to offer can shop at leipzig. de report and show on the map. Let it be colorful with your entry and show how great Leipzig is.
